About Grèce

Step into Grece, a Mediterranean-inspired sanctuary in Brisbane, masterfully designed for photo shoots, commercials, and intimate events. Every element of Grece has been meticulously curated with creativity in mind, from its natural light-drenched interiors to its stunning architectural details. The home is thoughtfully constructed to maximize the visual impact, with lighting that enhances every frame and backdrop, ensuring your project stands out. Whether you're shooting stills, video, or hosting a private event, Grece’s versatility brings beauty and depth to every moment.

Custom Shoot Areas

Designed specifically for photo and video shoots, Grèce offers adaptable spaces that cater to a range of creative projects, providing a wide range of diverse perfect backdrops every time.

Textured Surfaces

From rustic Mediterranean stone to sleek modern stucco, the textured finishes throughout Grèce add dimension and a rich, tactile quality to your visuals, elevating any aesthetic.

Natural Lighting

Expansive windows flood the space with natural light, highlighting the key areas for your shoot. The thoughtful architectural design ensures that lighting is a defining feature, creating professional-grade images effortlessly.

Wellness Space

Featuring a fully equipped wellness area and gym, Grèce is ideal for health and fitness shoots, offering an additional layer of versatility.

On-site Parking

With a spacious two-car garage, Grèce makes setup and pack-down seamless, allowing your crew to focus on the creative process.
